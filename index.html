About Dynamic Realities

Dynamic Realities is a Danish company founded in 2012. The mission is to develop fun, engaging and interesting experiences as well as tools that helps the creation of those experiences. The company is mostly a solo effort and is run by Mads Sønderstrup.

Privacy Policy

When registering for our product we collect and store your email, as well as any optional Personal Information you choose to share with us. By registering you also have the opportunity to opt-in to email correspondence sent to you from Dynamic Realities. Copies of any correspondence you send to Dynamic Realities may be kept for our records. Your email is collected only for product registration purposes and/or to keep you up-to-date on new products and changes to existing products.

Authorized third party websites that link to our products are not covered under our Privacy Policy and users must access such sites at their own risk.
